#### S-Video ####
Pin | Description
--- | ---
1 | Ground (Y)
2 | Ground (C)
3 | Luminance (Y)
4 | Chrominance (C)

#### Apple Desktop Bus ####
Pin | Description
--- | ---
1 | ADB bidirectional data
2 | Power switch
3 | Power +5V
4 | Ground
